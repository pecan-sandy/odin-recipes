Dirt Cake

Description

This dirt cake recipe is a great conversation piece at parties — adults love it as much as children do.
Garnish the flower pot cake with gummy worms; display it with a new garden trowel and artificial
flowers for the full effect.

Ingredients

  • 32 ounces chocolate sandwich cookies with creme filling
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 2 (3.5 ounce) packages instant vanilla pudding mix

Steps

  1. Finely crush chocolate cookies in a food processor; set crumbs aside.
  2. Combine cream cheese, butter, and confectioner's sugar in a large bowl; beat with an electric
    mixer until smooth.
  3. Whisk milk, whipped topping, and pudding together in a separate large bowl; fold in cream cheese
    mixture until well combined.
  4. Alternately layer crushed cookies and pudding mixture in a medium flower pot, starting and
    ending with cookies. Chill dirt cake in the refrigerator until ready to serve; garnish as desired.
